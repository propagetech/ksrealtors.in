ABOUT US

We have been in the construction business in Oman for the last 35 yrs and have a track record of successfully completing various commercial and residential projects for esteemed clientele in Oman. Our Director Mr K Satheesan, a veteran in the field of construction and a B.Sc-Civil Engineer from the College of Engineering , Trivandrum has an experience of 42 years in the Civil Construction Industry and is the founder of the company. Our core ethos is not just to meet customer expectations, but to exceed them, making us the partner customers want to work with.
KS REALTORS PVT LTD is an integrated real estate developer focused on premium developments with residential projects in Thiruvananthapuram. The brand reflects trade attributes of impeccable professionalism and bench mark of quality.

We aim to build inspirational development for our customers with distinctive design, quality construction and finishing. Everything we do is aimed at delighting our customers and ensuring their loyalty over the long term, surpassing customer’s expectations.

It is our vision to build the finest residential properties for the next generation – with spaces synonymous with inspirational architecture where creativity and happiness is nurtured.
We strongly believe that delivering projects is the first step in our long-lasting journey with our discerning residents, as our main task starts after possession of the homes. Providing residents with superior infrastructure, modern facilities that matches their aspirations, a safe and secure living environment and a refreshing ambience are the mainstay of our brand promise to our customers.

As part of our unflinching commitment to provide finest experience to customers, we consciously create homes sprinkled with hospitality to deliver optimum quality of life for our valuable customers.
